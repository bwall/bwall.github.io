Brian Wallace

Welcome! I'm Brian Wallace, but you can call me bwall. Working as a security researcher and software engineer, Github seems like the perfect place to launch a blog. I will write about development and research, and have a strong focus on botnet research.

Development

As an avid developer/engineer, I often have new code to post and share. Announcing new projects, discussing new techniques, etc.

Botnet Hunter

I do extensive botnet research, and take great pleasure in watching them burn. I will post details on botnets, their analyses, and their weaknesses. In some cases, I will host live tracking data here.

Research

Research is one of the most satisfying part of computer security. Finding new vulnerabilities, designing new attacks and developing exploits are just some of the things I’ll post on for security research.
